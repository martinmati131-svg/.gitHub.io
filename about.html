About Project Aura

Project Aura is a precision agriculture initiative based in Juja, Kenya. We bridge the gap between advanced robotics research and sustainable orchard management. Our mission is to empower local avocado growers with real-time, AI-driven data to optimize yields for exported Hass and Fuerte varieties.

The Visionary Behind the Tech

Developed by Martin Mati, a digital content creator and robotics researcher, Project Aura synthesizes years of experience in Edge-AI and hardware engineering. By leveraging the power of the Raspberry Pi 5 and ROS 2, we bring industrial-grade telemetry to the heart of the orchard.

Our Core Research

  • Predictive Intelligence: Using Juja-specific climate data to forecast Dry Matter (DM) accumulation.
  • Edge-AI Robotics: Implementing Vision-Language-Action models for automated crop monitoring.
  • Sustainable Scaling: Developing low-cost sensor arrays for small-to-medium scale farmers in the East African region.
