About Project Aura

Project Aura is a precision agriculture initiative based in Juja, Kenya. We bridge the gap between advanced robotics research and sustainable orchard management. Our mission is to empower local avocado growers with real-time, AI-driven data to optimize yields for exported Hass and Fuerte varieties.

The Visionary Behind the Tech

Developed by Martin Mati, a digital content creator and robotics researcher, Project Aura synthesizes years of experience in Edge-AI and hardware engineering. By leveraging the power of the Raspberry Pi 5 and ROS 2, we bring industrial-grade telemetry to the heart of the orchard.

Biological Integration: Beekeeping

We believe that technology should work in harmony with nature. A key component of our 2026 roadmap is the integration of African Honeybees (Apis mellifera scutellata). By monitoring hive activity alongside climate data, we ensure optimal cross-pollination between our Type A (Hass) and Type B (Fuerte) trees, maximizing fruit set and oil density.

Our Core Research Areas

  • Predictive Intelligence: Forecasting Dry Matter (DM) accumulation using local weather data.
  • Precision Pollination: Tracking bee flight windows to synchronize with flower opening cycles.
  • Edge-AI Robotics: Implementing Vision-Language-Action models for automated crop and hive monitoring.
